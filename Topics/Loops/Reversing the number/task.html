Reversing the number

Write a program that:

  1. Reads the integer from the stdin;
  2. Reverses its digits;
  3. Prints the result.

You can "shift" an integer to the left or to the right by multiplying or dividing it by 10:
1234 * 10 = 12340
1234 / 10 = 123

You can retrieve the last digit of an integer by taking the reminder of its division by 10:
1234 % 10 = 4


Sample Input:
1234567890

Sample Output:
987654321



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
